Airline Passenger Survey Analysis

7/30/2022

Executive summary.

According to the models, customers overall satisfaction seem to be influenced heavily by Seat Comfort satisfaction, Departure Delay, and Arrival Delay. Overall there doesn’t seem to be a relationship with check-in service (A12) and baggage handling (A23). Additionally, no statistical significance between the classes with the food-and-drink satisfaction.A majority of customers are Loyal or returning, youth to middle age old, and travel medium haul flights. Surprisingly, the arrival and departure of flights are delayed less than 15 minutes.

According to attribute selection, Type.of.Travel, Class, Inflight.wifi.service, Online.boarding Inflight.entertainment and Checkin.service seems to influence the overall satisfaction the most. Arrival Delay and Departure Delay is contested in attribute selection, appearing important in one but not the other. Gender appears to be the least important feature with relations to overall satisfaction or any data other features.
